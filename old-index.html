technicalities:

photo path game!.

Let's make a photo-path. What's a photo path? It's a sequence of images that visually and spatially leads from one to the other. I'll start by taking 3-10 pictures that lead you away from campus. Then I'll send an email to someone in the class and ask them to continue the chain/path. That person will nominate another person and son on and we'll see how far we can get. Try to include visual clues in your photos to keep people on the right path! But also, take pictures that offer a little bit of a challenge.

After you contribute 3-5 images to the chain, post them to tumblr and send an email to the next person in the chain, letting them know to start. You can't tell them where to start! They have to work through the chain.
